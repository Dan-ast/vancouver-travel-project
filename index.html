Why Vancouver?

🏙️ A Thriving Urban Playground

Nestled between the Pacific Ocean and the Coastal Mountains, Vancouver offers surreal access to hiking trails, ski slopes, and beaches - all within city limits. Kayak at sunrise in Deep Cove or watch the sunset from Spanish Banks!

From the historic charm of Gastown's cobblestone streets to the futuristic skyline of Yaletown, Vancouver blends old-world elegance with modern vibrancy. Don't miss Granville Island's artisan markets or Robson Street's global cuisine.

Stanley Park

A 1,000-acre urban oasis with ancient cedars, the iconic seawall, and totem poles at Brockton Point.

Address:

Vancouver, BC V6G 1Z4, Canada

What I Like About It

🌳 The Seawall: Biking or walking this 9km oceanfront loop feels like therapy-salt air, mountain views and eagle sightings included!
🦝 Wildlife Surprises: Spotting raccoons, herons, and even seals from the shore never gets old.
🌲 Secret Forest Trails: Wandering off-path to find 800-year-old cedars in the quiterer corners of the park.
🚂 The Miniature Train: Nostalgic fun (especially during Halloween's "Ghost Train" event!).
🌊 Beach Breaks: Third Beach's sunset picnics are my happy place.

Capilano Suspension Bridge

Walk 140 meters above the rainforest canopy on this thrilling bridge - don't miss the Treetops Adventure!

Address:

3735 Capilano Rd, North Vancouver, BC V7R 4J1, Canada

What I Like About It

🌉 Adrenaline Rush: Walking 140 meters above the canyon floor while the bridge sways - my heart still pounds thinking about it!
🌲 Treetops Adventures: Climbing through a rainforest canopy on wooden walkways feels like being in the "Avatar" movie.
📸 Epic Photos: The Cliffwalk's glass platforms give dizzing views (perfect for Instagram bravery).

Queen Elizabeth Park

Vancouver's highest point offers stunning skyline views, the Bloedel Conservatory, and quarry gardens.

Address:

Vancouver, BC V5Y 2N3, Canada

What I Like About It

🌸 Bloedel Conservatory: A tropical paradise under a glass dome-home to exotic birds and lush plants, even in winter!
🎾 Sports & Rec: Tennis courts, lawn bowling, and disc golf—all with a view.
☕ Seasons in the Park: The restaurant's patio offers cozy fireside dining in fall and cherry blossom views in spring.
👨‍👩‍👧 Kid-Friendly Fun: Rolling hills for picnics, duck ponds, and the giant chessboard near the conservatory!
🍁 Seasonal Highlights: Spring cherry blossoms, summer concerts, and fall foliage walks.
